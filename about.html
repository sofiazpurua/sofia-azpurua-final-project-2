The Guitar's History

The guitar has a long and interesting history, dating back to the 15th century. It evolved from earlier stringed instruments and became a favorite in many music genres including classical, rock, blues, and folk.

Why Guitar?

With its wide range of sounds and versatility, the guitar is perfect for beginners and advanced musicians alike. It allows you to play multiple music styles and express yourself throuhg cords and strumming patterns.
